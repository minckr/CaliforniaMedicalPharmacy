## Suggestions based on comparable DME/CRT + pharmacy businesses

Not built yet, just flagging for you to prioritize:

1. **Physician/provider referral form.** Nearly universal on CRT sites (a
   fillable PDF or web form referring providers use to start a wheelchair
   evaluation, fax-in or online). Worth having a dedicated referral fax/
   email, separate from the general contact form.
2. **Accreditation display, once confirmed.** DMEPOS Medicare billing
   requires accreditation from a CMS-approved body: ACHC, NABP (built for
   pharmacies specifically), CHAP, or The Compliance Team. If you're
   already accredited, that badge belongs on the About or home page, it's
   a real trust signal in this industry. If not yet accredited, it's worth
   knowing this is likely required for DME/CRT Medicare billing.
3. **Bilingual site toggle (EN/ES).** You mentioned a large Spanish-speaking
   patient base; an actual language toggle, not just a mention in the About
   copy, would likely matter more to patients than the paragraph does.
4. **Insurance verification / "get started" form**, separate from general
   contact, so patients or providers can submit insurance info directly
   rather than only calling.
5. **Service area** (which LA neighborhoods/counties you deliver to) if
   delivery range is limited, worth stating plainly so people don't call to
   ask.
6. **HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices** as its own linked document
   (separate from the website Privacy Policy, which only covers the SMS/
   web side).
